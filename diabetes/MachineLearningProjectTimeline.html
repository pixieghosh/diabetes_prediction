Machine Learning 2023 Project Timeline

Manpreet Katari

For the final project, students are required to read the primary literature, identify a biological problem and the available datasets to work with (these data may be your own), and undertake a computational analysis to tackle the problem.

For their final report, students will generate written report of their study (a ipynb file exported as a .html) and present their projects to instructors and fellow students.

For all written proposal components and presentations please submit a single file via Brightspace.

  1. Title
  2. Table of contents
  3. Abstract

  5. Hypothesis
  6. Sufficient background for non-expert reader
  7. Motivation for problem addressed
  8. Presentation of Results
  9. Interpretation of Results
  10. Discussion/Conclusion

  12. Clear Statement of Future work
